Add players to compare

Fangraphs allows you to add as many players as you want, as long as the players are in the Fangraphs database.

About this task

The Fangraphs compare tool can be used for many purposes, but the main reason is for comparing multiple players over a specific time period.

Procedure

  1. Have something in mind beforehand that you want to compare.
  2. Go to fangraphs.com.
  3. Search for your player in the searchbar.
  4. Go to their profile page.
  5. Select compare on the right hand side.
  6. Choose your parameters from the dropdown menu.
  7. Add your players that you want to compare.
  8. Click create comparison.

Results

After following these steps, you should see multiple charts displaying your choice of parameters over your time period.
